3.Impact Vision Reflection

My Core Values: Empathy and Fairness

Approach to Work: Analytical, Strategic, and Creative
When tackling tasks, I believe in being both analytical and strategic. These two strengths are fundamental to my creativity. I think that creativity isn't just about spontaneous ideas; it’s about carefully analyzing a problem, understanding its context, and strategically navigating the best solutions.

Influence on Entertainment
Entertainment has a profound impact on culture, and in the US, it plays a crucial role in shaping public perception and satisfaction. Americans enjoy high-quality, easily accessible, and diverse entertainment options that contribute to a sense of collective pleasure. However, in many countries, including parts of China and other developing regions, entertainment options are limited, and people often struggle to fulfill their desires for enjoyment.

I aim to focus on creating efficient entertainment content that can reach and resonate with people from all walks of life. I believe this is not only about providing entertainment but also about fairness—ensuring that those who might not have had access to diverse forms of entertainment, especially people in anxious or underserved communities, can also experience joy and fulfillment. I sense that many in the US have access to greater emotional and social power through entertainment, and I want to help extend this power globally, making entertainment accessible to those who need it most.

Career Development and Next Steps
In the upcoming semester, I plan to dive deep into UX design and user research. I believe that understanding user needs and behavior is essential for creating meaningful content that resonates. I’m excited to learn how to apply these skills to improve user experiences and further my ability to design entertainment content that’s not only efficient but impactful.

4.Reflect on Your Purpose and Values:

My professional purpose is deeply aligned with my core values of empathy and fairness. I believe these values enable me to truly understand diverse user needs while striving to create inclusive and equitable solutions. They shape my approach to design, pushing me to prioritize accessibility, user-centricity, and meaningful impact in all my work.

My career goal is to enhance global access to high-quality entertainment content. I am passionate about creating user experiences that provide joy and relaxation, particularly for communities with limited options. Entertainment has the power to connect people, uplift spirits, and reduce anxiety. By focusing on efficient and strategic content design, I hope to bring these benefits to a broader audience.

In my community, I see myself as a connector and an advocate for fairness. I aim to bridge the gap between technology and human needs, ensuring that innovative solutions are both accessible and valuable to everyone. This vision drives me to approach every challenge strategically and analytically while maintaining a deep sense of empathy for those I serve. By staying true to my values, I aspire to create work that not only meets immediate user needs but also fosters long-term positive impact.
